# Pump Portable Design System

## Visual Direction

Premium Swedish outdoor-tech.

The site should feel like a modern product brand, not a generic Shopify template.

Inspiration:

- Apple-like clarity
- Tesla-like product confidence
- premium outdoor gear
- Scandinavian minimalism
- water, air, freedom and clean surfaces

## Color Palette

Recommended palette:

- Deep Navy: #071014
- Charcoal: #101820
- Surface: #14212A
- Surface Light: #1D2B35
- Mist: #F6F7F2
- Muted Text: #9AA8A2
- Water Blue: #4CC9F0
- Nordic Teal: #2DD4BF
- Warm Sand: #E8D8B9
- Offer Amber: #F59E0B
- Border: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)

## Layout Principles

- Large hero with immediate product clarity.
- Strong above-the-fold CTA.
- Use product imagery as the star.
- Use dark premium surfaces with bright product contrast.
- Use rounded cards sparingly.
- Avoid cluttered Shopify default grids.
- Mobile-first spacing.

## Components

Prioritize reusable section patterns:

- announcement bar
- sticky header
- hero
- use-case cards
- benefit cards
- product buy box
- comparison table/cards
- testimonial cards
- FAQ accordion
- sticky mobile CTA
- footer

## Motion

Motion should be subtle:

- fade/slide on scroll if lightweight
- hover lift on cards
- gentle image reveal
- no heavy animation libraries unless approved

## Performance

- Avoid large JS.
- Avoid autoplay-heavy sections.
- Lazy-load images below the fold.
- Prefer CSS scroll areas instead of carousel libraries.
- Optimize for mobile first.
