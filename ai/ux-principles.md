# UX Principles

## Above The Fold

Within 5 seconds, the visitor should understand:

- what the product is
- when they would use it
- why it is better than not having one
- what to click next

## Homepage Flow

Recommended homepage order:

1. Announcement bar: free shipping / offer
2. Header
3. Premium hero with product and CTA
4. Use cases: camping, boat, sauna, beach, vanlife, dog
5. Featured combo product
6. Benefits
7. How it works
8. Comparison
9. Reviews
10. FAQ
11. Final CTA
12. Footer

## Product Page Flow

Recommended product page order:

1. Product media + buy box
2. Key benefits close to price/CTA
3. Use cases
4. Specs
5. What's included
6. Reviews/trust
7. FAQ
8. Related products

## Conversion Rules

- Keep the main CTA obvious.
- Put trust signals near the CTA.
- Explain the Perfect Combo clearly.
- Make mobile purchase flow very easy.
- Reduce choice overload.
- Highlight use cases visually.
